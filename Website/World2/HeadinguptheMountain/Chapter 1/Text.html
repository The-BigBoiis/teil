Chapter 1: Negotiations

"Six hundred," he sais while slapping a big bag on the table. "Half's right here, the other half you'll get after the Job's done." Not a bad offer, but I probably can't trust the guy. His shiny but cheap looking armor gives off major scammer vibes. Also, wearing a helmet inside reeks. Chances are the gold inside that dirty bag is counterfeit.

"Do you mind if i take a closer look at the three hundred gold then?" Showing off my quick math skills like that almost never fails to intimidate. I'm not sure how he reacted though, since i can't see his face behind that helmet. Even the eyes, which would usually be visible, are hidden beneath darkness.

Here our story begins.
